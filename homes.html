About UNIFIND

Our Mission

The CSPC Lost and Found Management System is a centralized, web-based platform designed to serve the Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges community. Our mission is simple: to efficiently and effectively reunite students, faculty, and staff with their lost belongings.

We understand that losing a personal item can be stressful. This system is built to make the process of reporting, finding, and recovering lost items as smooth, transparent, and quick as possible. We aim to foster a campus culture of honesty, responsibility, and community support, where every member feels confident that a lost item has a high chance of being returned.

OUR POLICIES
Please read our official guidelines. This ensures that your lost items are handled properly, safely, and with correct facility adherence.

Holding Period

Found items will be held in safekeeping by the Office of Student Affairs for a period of 30 days (one month).

Unclaimed Items

Items that remain unclaimed after the 30-day holding period may be recycled, donated, or disposed of in accordance with school policies.

Disclaimer

The CSPC Lost and Found Management System is a service provided to the community. CSPC is not responsible for any lost, stolen, or damaged items. Users are encouraged to follow all community reminders to ensure the retrieval of their belongings.
